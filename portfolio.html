The Stella Manor

Stella Manor: A Contemporary Luxury Residential Project

Stella Manor is a high-end residential project located in Abuja, designed to merge modern architectural principles with sustainable living solutions. This project demonstrates the firm’s commitment to precision, functionality, and long-term value.

Architectural Approach

The design incorporates open-plan layouts, extensive use of natural light, and seamless integration with the surrounding landscape. Floor-to-ceiling windows, locally sourced stone, and glass panels define the building’s contemporary aesthetic.

Sustainability Features

Stella Manor incorporates sustainable systems, including solar power integration, rainwater collection, and advanced insulation materials. These features ensure energy efficiency and reduce environmental impact, aligning with modern green building standards.
